Company History

My pizza has been a blessing to this community since we opened in 2005. Xavier's Pizza has become a favorite among the locals because of the freshest ingredients, the best hand tossed dough and our signature taste. Whether you need a slice of pepperoni or just a plain cheese pizza. You will be satisfied no matter what style of pie you like.

In 2005 we opened with one simple mission to make handmade great pizza that brings people together. A small shop in the neighborhood turned into a local institution because of our fresh house made dough daily and just right sauces. Xavier's Pizza started out small but grew into so much more than just a restaurant. It's a place where friends and family and neighbors could gather, get to know each other, share great food and make great memories.
